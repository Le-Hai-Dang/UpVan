Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal information is our top priority. This "Privacy Policy" applies to UpVan and the information we collect and how we use it.

COLLECT INFORMATION INCLUDES:

  • Personal Information Collected Directly from Users: Users on the Application UpVan may be asked to fill in certain profile information Personal profile is required according to the form information fields available on the App UpVan to use the service such as: Full name, gender, date of birth, email address,... This information is required to complete your participation in the service as a User
  • Information collected directly when Users use the application: Users when using the Application UpVan, Application UpVan may collect some technical data such as: location, IP address, access duration/session, access device, information information about access device such as operating system, manufacturer, model…

The main data collection on the application includes: : Full name, gender, date of birth, email address... This is the information that the Application UpVan is required by User to:

  • To process and assist the User in the User's service requests.
  • To contact the User and send information related to the use of the application.
  • To send marketing messages and offers to Users.
  • To notify Users of application updates or changes to the service provided.
  • To handle and respond to questions, complaints and feedback from Users.
  • For feedback on app usage and administration, support, and improvement of performance, User experience, and app functions.
  • To enable us to comply with our obligations as required by a competent state agency in conducting investigations, responding to questions or instructions as required by law.
  • To prevent, detect and investigate any prohibited, illegal, unauthorized or fraudulent activity.
  • To ensure compliance with legal regulations.
  • To share location, map with friends.

SCOPE OF INFORMATION USE:

  • To contact the User to confirm the service when receiving a request from the User
  • To provide information about the service to the User if there is a request from the User
  • To send marketing emails, introducing services that feel relevant to Users
  • To send notifications about app activities
  • To communicate and resolve complaints with Users
  • To contact and deal with Users in special cases

INFORMATION STORAGE TIME:

  • User's personal data will be stored until requested by Application Management to destroy.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

UpVan will regularly update this privacy policy to reflect customer feedback. UpVan recommends that you periodically review the policy this to stay informed about how UpVan is protecting your information.
